NAACCR XML: Tumor.uihoFacility

Alternate Names
   Urban Indian Health Organization City
Description
The UIHO City data item is derived using NAACCR*Prep based on the state and county at the time of diagnosis and includes UIHO facilities that receive Title 5 funding from IHS and Urban Indian Health Network sites identified through the Urban Indian Health Institute CDC BRANCH grant.
