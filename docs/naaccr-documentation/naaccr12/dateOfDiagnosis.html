Date of Initial Diagnosis (CoC) 390 8 SEER/CoC 530-537

Description
Date of initial diagnosis by a recognized medical practitioner for the tumor being reported whether clinically or microscopically confirmed. See page 99 for date format.

For more discussion on determining date of diagnosis, consult the SEER Program Coding and Staging Manual or CoC FORDS manual.
