NAACCR XML: Tumor.pdl1

Description

The absence or presence of PD-L1 expression determines if the tumor will respond to treatment with a targeted inhibitor (immunotherapy). PD-L1 is done for Non-small cell lung cancers (NSCLC).

Rationale

PD-L1 is recommended by treatment guidelines for lung cancer to determine if the patient may benefit from checkpoint inhibitor drugs (immunotherapy). It is a new data item for cases diagnosed 1/1/2025+.
