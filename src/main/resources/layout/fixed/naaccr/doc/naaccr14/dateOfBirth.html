Birth Date(SEER/CoC/CCCR)
 240 8 SEER/CoC 196 - 203
Description
Date of birth of the patient. See Chapter X for date format. If age at diagnosis and year of diagnosis are known, but year of birth is unknown, then year of birth should be calculated and so coded. Only the year should be entered, left-justified. Estimate date of birth when information is not available. It is better to estimate than to leave birthdate unknown.
