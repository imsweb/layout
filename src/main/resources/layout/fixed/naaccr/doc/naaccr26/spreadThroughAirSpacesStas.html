NAACCR XML: Tumor.spreadThroughAirSpacesStas

Description

Spread Through Air Spaces (STAS) is defined as micropapillary clusters, solid nests, or single cells of tumor extending beyond the edge of the tumor into the air spaces of the surrounding lung parenchyma.

Rationale

Initial studies have shown that the presence of STAS is associated with an increased incidence of recurrence in tumors that have undergone limited resection (e.g., segmentectomy, wedge resection).
