NAACCR XML: Tumor.radLocationOfRx

Alternate Names
   Location of Radiation Treatment (CoC)
Description

Identifies the location of the facility where radiation treatment was administered during first course of treatment. See also RX Summ--Radiation [1360].

Codes

0No radiation treatment
1All radiation treatment at this facility
2Radiation started at reporting facility, continued elsewhere
3Radiation started elsewhere, continues at this facility
4All radiation treatment elsewhere
8Other, NOS
9Unknown
