NAACCR XML: Tumor.oncotypeDxRecurrenceScoreDcis

Description
Oncotype Dx Recurrence Score-DCIS is a numeric score of a genomic test to predict the risk of local recurrence of breast cancer based on the assessment of 12 genes.
Rationale
Oncotype Dx Recurrence Score-DCIS is a Registry Data Collection Variable in AJCC. It is a new data item for cases diagnosed 1/1/2018+.
